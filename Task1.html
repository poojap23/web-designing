HOLY CROSS HOSPITAL

An attempt to trace the genesis of this great centre of health care leads us to the “Golden Age” of the Travancore rulers. His Highness Sree Moolam Thirunal Ramavarma, the Maharaja of Travancore was noted for his benevolent humane and great concern for his subjects. Hence the Maharaja:1500 Maharaja sought the help of the then Bishop Aloysius Maria Benziger of Quilon in getting missionary sisters for the Government hospitals in Kerala. In response to this request, the Bishop a Swiss by nationality, gave the distress call to the Holy Cross Sisters from Menzingen, a town in Switzerland Switzerland. The Holy Cross Sisters came to Kerala in 1906. Four of them served in the District Hospital, Kollam while four others joined the General Hospital, Trivandrum. In the following years, many more sisters from Switzerland, Italy, Germany and England joined the mission.

College of Nursing at Kottiyam was a dream of our pioneer members for advancing nursing education which they had begun as the first of its kind in Kerala. The College was started on 3rd March 2003 by admitting the first batch of Post Basic BSc Nursing students. Four Year BSc Nursing programme was started in the year 2004 with an intake of 50 students. The institute aims at conducting nursing education upholding catholic doctrines. The College is recognized by INC and KNC and affiliated to the University of Kerala, Thiruvananthapuram. The College started to be viable with full function from its very beginning with curricular and extracurricular activities under the expert leadership.
